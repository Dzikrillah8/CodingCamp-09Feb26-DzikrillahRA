About Us

SM Entertainment is a South Korean entertainment company founded in 1995 by Lee Soo-man. Based in Seoul, the company is widely recognized as a pioneer of the modern K-pop industry and has played a significant role in spreading the Korean Wave (Hallyu) worldwide.


As a leading artist management agency, SM Entertainment represents globally known groups and artists such as EXO, Red Velvet, NCT, and aespa. With its structured training system and strong emphasis on creative innovation, SM Entertainment continues to deliver influential music and content to audiences around the world.
